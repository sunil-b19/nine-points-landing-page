BINS THE WAY TO GO

Menstruation Dustbin Services: Recognizing the importance of proper menstrual waste disposal, we provide menstruation dustbin services across Nepal. By installing strategically located bins and implementing effective waste management systems, we ensure safe and hygienic disposal of menstrual waste, contributing to environmental sustainability.

Our Impact

"Empowering Women in Nepal”

Since our inception, Ninenty has made significant strides in advancing female empowerment and menstrual health management in Nepal. We have reached thousands of women and girls with our educational programs, empowered communities to break the silence around menstruation, and established sustainable systems for menstrual waste management.

1000+ women’s impacted

At Ninenty, we believe that together, we can create a world where every woman and girl has the opportunity to realize her full potential. Join us in our mission to empower, educate, and inspire change. Together, we can build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
